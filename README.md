## How to Customize
1. Open `script.js` and replace the password variable with your own.
2. Edit the love letter text in the `.love-letter` section of `index.html`.
3. Replace the images in the `images/flowers/` folder with your own.
4. In `musicPlaylist[]` inside `script.js`, insert your songs or YouTube embed links.
